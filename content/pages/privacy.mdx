# Privacy Policy

Privacy matters. I have built this site to collect as little data about you as possible — ideally none.
Here is the honest picture of what happens when you visit.

*Last reviewed: May 2026*

---

## The Short Version

This site does **not** collect your personal data. There are no accounts, no tracking pixels,
no analytics packages following you around the web, and no cookies beyond what is strictly
necessary to remember your theme preference (light or dark mode).

---

## What This Site Does Not Do

- Does not collect your name, email address, or any personal information
- Does not use third-party advertising or ad-tracking services
- Does not fingerprint your browser or device
- Does not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties
- Does not require an account to read any content

---

## Hosting & Infrastructure

This site is hosted on **Vercel**. Like any web host, Vercel's servers receive standard HTTP
request metadata — IP address, browser user agent, and the requested URL — as a normal part
of serving web pages. This is Vercel's infrastructure data, not mine. See
[Vercel's Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy) for how they handle server logs.

---

## Theme Preference

If you switch between light and dark mode, that preference is saved in your browser's `localStorage`.
This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to me or anyone else.

---

## External Links

This site links to external resources — GitHub, documentation, and other references.
Once you follow a link off this site, you are subject to that site's own privacy practices.
I have no control over what external sites collect.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If anything meaningful changes about how this site handles data, I will update this page and
the review date above. Given the minimal nature of the site, significant changes are unlikely.
