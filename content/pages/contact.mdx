## Ways to Reach Me

### General Questions

Email is the most reliable path. I check it regularly and reply when I can.
No question is too basic — if something here confused you, that is useful feedback.

### Bug Reports & Corrections

Found something broken, out of date, or just plain wrong?
Please tell me. Open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/mithun3) or drop me an email
with the page URL and what needs fixing. I would much rather know than not know.

### Content Suggestions

Is there something you want to see covered that is not here yet? A topic, a technology, a pattern
you have been searching for good notes on? Let me know. No promises, but if it is something I know
well or am actively curious about, there is a reasonable chance I will write it up.

### Collaboration

Interested in working on something together? Reach out with details.
I am particularly interested in projects at the intersection of software, infrastructure,
music technology, and outdoor or field computing.
